Contrast
Gymshark
gymshark.com

This webpage shows the design element of contrast with the colors they used in relation to the text and the background. The consistent black logo with the white background, and then with the dark image having white letters on the dark green grass. The button for shop now is surrounded with white space, and makes the "shop now" stand out so much more because it is so light compared to the dark surroundings.

Visual Hierarchy
Plasma Donation Center
biolifeplasma.com

This webpage shows visual hierarchy with the different emphasis that is put on the different important pieces of text. First, the eyes are naturally drawn to the bold letters saying "New Donors Earn up to $975" which is their main message they want to get across to their audience. Then, the eye moves to the image where it repeats the amount of money you will earn if you donate. The large text is what draws the attention there. Next, the last big element in the line of hierarchy is the "Sign up" button that is surrounded with white space and is dark blue contrasting with the background to stand out. These three elements in order of their hierarchy show that there is more emphasis that the company wants to put on certain spots on the website.

Alignment
Subway
order.subway.com

The Subway website looks very neat and organized due to the left alignment of all the similar text. The text is consistent throughout the page and a clear line can be drawn where the margin of the text is. The lines that the text make keep the page organized and makes it easier for the eye to read left to right. The alignment of the "Order now" button is center-aligned with the image to be consistent with what is surrounding it, and so there is more of a central focus and it draws the audience in to make an order. This was stratigically placed in line with the sandwiches to correlate the sandwich to what will be ordered.
