Wedding Reception

If you are planning a wedding reception, Temple Inn & Suites is the perfect place to host all of your family close to your designated temple. This family-friendly atmosphere is the best place to host both sides of your family from all ages. There is a shuttle to the temple so your family does not have to stress about transportation. At all of our locations, we have many different sized rooms and wide open space to host your reception if you desire. Our purpose is to serve you, and no matter what we want this special day to be memorable and as stress free as possible.

Reception Set Up

If you don't already have a venue in town, don't worry. We have multiple lounge areas on every level that are large enough to host your reception. We will set up and take down the tables and chairs to relieve the after party hassle. Decorations are 100% up to you and your taste.
