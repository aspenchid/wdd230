Temple Highlight

The Salt Lake Temple began construction in 1853 when the Pioneers used oxen to haul mammoth blocks of granite 15 miles down a canyon and across the valley. The building site was dedicated on April 6th, 1893. The ground breaking was on February 14th, 1853, and was finally announced on July 28th, 1847. The temple and grounds are currently under construction, but visitors are welcome to peruse the grounds and admire the stunning workmanship of the building and the serene beauty of its immediate surroundings.

About us

The Temple Inn & Suites is a hotel chain headquartered in Bethesda, MD that builds and manages specialized hotel accommodations near temple locations of The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints around the world. The owner-operated company caters to temple workers, patrons, and their families by providing specialized services and accommodations that meet the needs of those patrons. The hotel accommodations and services are available to anyone willing to abide by the terms and policies outlined by the company. The overall goal is to meet the needs of temple patrons who come to serve in the temple or who participate in events such as sealings, weddings, receptions, and youth trips. This business reflects the Church culture and is designed to support a temple oriented theme. Temple Inn & Suites is a haven of comfort and accessibility.
